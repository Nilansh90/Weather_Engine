Hierarchical Intelligence

Rather than predicting every atmospheric variable independently, Weather Engine combines multiple specialised machine learning models into a dependency-aware forecasting hierarchy. Lower-level predictions become engineered features for higher tiers, producing forecasts that remain both statistically accurate and physically consistent.
