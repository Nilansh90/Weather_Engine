# Final Milestones

When we finish, your project won't be judged as "a weather forecasting app." It will read as a complete machine learning systems project. A recruiter should be able to recognize, in order:

1. A production-style ML inference engine with hierarchical models.
2. Automated data ingestion, feature engineering, evaluation, and reporting.
3. A polished engineering dashboard that exposes the system's internals rather than hiding them.
4. Strong software engineering practices through architecture, reusable components, automation, and deployment.
5. An end-to-end product that demonstrates data engineering, ML engineering, backend development, frontend development, databases, visualization, and operational thinking.

At that point, the website itself becomes evidence of your engineering ability, not just a wrapper around your models. That's the standard I'd aim for with the remaining work.
