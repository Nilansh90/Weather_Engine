Workflow Until Deployment
The pipeline can be executed daily by running:
python scripts/master_script.py
or by executing it directly in an IDE.

The automated sequence runs as follows:
Recharge -> Fetch Tomorrow's NWP -> Predict -> Calculate Errors -> Email Report

Manual intervention for individual scripts is no longer required.

Workflow After Deployment
The process becomes fully automated:
Cron Job (at 00:00) -> master_script.py -> Fully automated execution

Overall Progress Estimate

* Backend and ML Engine: 95 to 98 percent complete
* Entire project (including Flask, deployment, and documentation): 80 to 85 percent complete

The complex engineering tasks (designing the DAG, training the models, integrating the database,
building the prediction pipeline, and automating the workflow) are largely finished.
The remaining work primarily involves presenting the data (Flask UI), deploying the application, and writing
